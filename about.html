music that lives between midnight and 4am

mayjune is a DJ and producer crafting late-night soundscapes that blur the line between memory and movement. Drawing from deep house, melodic techno, and ambient textures, every set is a journey built for the dancefloor and the long drive home.

The sound started taking shape in small venues and dive bars — intimate rooms where the music could breathe. From those early sets came a signature: slow builds, emotional drops, and an atmosphere that makes time disappear.

Whether it's a headline set, a studio session, or a late-night b2b, mayjune brings the same intention every time — music that actually means something.
